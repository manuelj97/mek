LORENZO THE RUNNER

CREATIVE DIRECTING

JANUARY 20, 2024

VIEW LIVE PROJECT

The Idea for making Lorenzo the Runner emerged from my love for video-games.
Drawn to the classic platformer genre I adored in my youth,
I decided to return to my roots and create one myself.

I led the game's creative direction, assigning tasks to freelancers,
organizing their deliveries and integrating them to create the final product

The illustrations were created by a talented artist Maria Del Pilar,
who brought my ideas to the pixel canvas.

The project was successfully completed and released on online game distribution portals.
